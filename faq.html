Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ? You need to go to https://print.hz.nl/, log in to your hz accout, upload the files to be printed, confirm printing settings, next you go to a printer, scan your card, chose the printing option and the documents should print.
How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event on Friday 16/09) on the HZ webshop? Go to https://webshop.hz.nl/, log in to your hz account and then you will be able to purchase whatever you want.
Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID-19? https://hz.nl/en/current-affairs/coronavirus
How can you book a project space in one of the wings? You can make a reservation at the Self Service Portal depending on availabiliity @ https://hzuniversity.topdesk.net/tas/public/ssp/.
What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot? All you need to do is show your hz pass at the barrier.
